Network Domain — 5G, Fiber JV & Strategic Initiatives
Vodafone Germany: Europe's largest B2B telecom + second-largest mobile operator in Germany. Key initiatives: 5G densification, OXG fiber JV, enterprise private networks.
Mobile sub decline reflects competition from 1&1 (new entrant), O2/TEF, and Telekom. Broadband steady; OXG fiber JV to address fixed growth gap.
Stock shows Vodafone Group (parent VOD) vs European telecom peers. Germany represents ~35% of Group service revenue. VOD undergoes portfolio transformation (Vantage Towers, Indus Towers).
